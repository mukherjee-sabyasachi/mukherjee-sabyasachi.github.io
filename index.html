About me

Welcome to my webpage! I am primarily interested in analytic number theory and automorphic forms. Recently, I have been drawn to questions surrounding the sphere packing problems in various directions. I shall be doing a PhD at the University of Cologne under the supervision of Prof. Sander Zwegers from April 2026. Back in 2022, I graduated with an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Georg-August-Universität Göttingen. My M.Sc. thesis is here.
